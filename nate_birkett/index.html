My Specialties

Nature Photography

Nature photography covers shooting flora and fauna. This means plants, flowers, and animals, photographed in their immediate environments. It's challenging because of the variety of subjects and movement.

Portrait Photography

Portrait photography or portraiture in photography is a photograph of a person or group of people that captures the personality of the subject by using effective lighting, backdrops, and poses.

Wedding Photography

Wedding photography involves taking photographs of wedding ceremonies and participants. However, most professionals realize that there is much more to wedding photography than simply snapping a few pictures.

Food & Drink Photography

Food photography is a still life photography genre used to create attractive still life photographs of food.
